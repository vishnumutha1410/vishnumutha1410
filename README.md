## About Me

I'm a **Data Engineer & Data Analyst** focused on building reliable data pipelines and turning raw data into clear, actionable insights. I'm currently pursuing my **MS in Computer Science (Data Analytics)** at Webster University, building on a BTech in Computer Science.

I work across the full data pipeline — from cleaning and modeling data, to transforming it, to presenting results that people can act on.

- 🔭 Currently building end-to-end data projects across analytics and engineering
- 🌱 Deepening my skills in **ETL pipelines, Snowflake, and data modeling**
- 📊 I've analyzed 180,000+ IPL ball-by-ball records and 8,800+ Netflix titles
- 🎓 Google Data Analytics certified
- 💬 Ask me about Python, SQL, data warehousing, or dashboards
